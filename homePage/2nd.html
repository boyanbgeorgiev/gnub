About CoralAI

CoralAI is a groundbreaking project at the intersection of technology and marine conservation. Our mission is to protect and preserve coral reefs worldwide through the application of cutting-edge artificial intelligence.

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate scientists and technologists, CoralAI aims to revolutionize coral reef conservation efforts by providing innovative solutions for monitoring, analysis, and decision-making.

Through our advanced image recognition AI, we empower researchers, conservationists, and policymakers to gain valuable insights into the health and status of coral reefs, enabling more effective conservation actions.
